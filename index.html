FAQs

I’ve seen other Apps that serve similar purposes. What makes yours the best?

Unlike any other app, iLightShow is not only listening to your favorite device's microphone. Actually, it's based on top of Spotify Analysis services so that it knows in advance how to sync lights with music's beats in order to create a perfectly synced light show!

How do I sync iLightShow with Spotify?

You know what? It's super easy.

 

macOS

You just need to open the Spotify Desktop Client and log in with your credentials. iLightShow will automatically communicate with Spotify and it handles all the rest for you.

 

iOS / Android

You just need to log in with your Spotify account from iLightShow App. Then, iLightShow will automatically sync the lights on top of the music's beats by regularly fetching Spotify services. Note that you need to have a reliable internet connection so that iLightShow can process Spotify data in real-time.

 

Is iLightShow compatible with Apple Music?

Yes it is compatible with iLightShow for iOS. The compatibility with the macOS version will come soon.
