Sync Lights to Music

Sync your Philips Hue, LIFX and Nanoleaf lights to Spotify, Apple Music and more 🎉

Sync Philips Hue, LIFX and Nanoleaf lights to Spotify, Apple Music and more. iLightShow automatically creates a light show that is synchronized with your favorite songs. You can customize the light show with a variety of options to create the perfect experience for any occasion.

iOS & Android versions

This website is dedicated to iLightShow 3 for iOS. For the Android version, visit this article.

Features

Lights

Control your Philips Hue, LIFX or Nanoleaf (panels only, essentials aren’t supported)

Music

Sync your lights to Apple Music, Spotify or to the Microphone input

Advanced Effects

  • Beats and Drums detection
  • Strobe on beatdrops
  • Automatically responds to changes in mood and energy
  • Multiple options for color palettes and effects

Visit the Effects modes page for more information

Presets, Shortcuts

Create presets from iLightShow and launch them with iOS Shortcuts

Auto-Calibration

Automatically calibrate your lights to your music using the microphone input on iOS

Requirements

  • iOS 16.0 or later
  • Philips Hue bridge, LIFX or Nanoleaf panels
  • An active subscription for Apple Music or Spotify is required to sync your lights to music from these services
